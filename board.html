Thomas Hess — President

Thomas Hess serves as the President and is a member of the board of directors. Tom is responsible for managing all aspects of the Global Higher Education strategic plan, including launch planning, fundraising campaign, social media and marketing, government relations, and advisory board selection. Tom is also accountable for advancing the major gifts program, which encompasses the successful identification, cultivation, solicitation, and retention of major gift and planned gift prospects.

Tom brings over 24 years of Executive Leadership in the global pharmaceutical industry. His diverse background includes executive level positions in sales leadership, operations, brand management, innovation, strategic planning and customer engagement marketing. Education: Tom was educated at Xavier University, University of Chicago Booth CMI and has his MBA with a focus in Global Business from Lake Forest Graduate School of Management. He is also obtained the designation of Hotchkiss Scholar.

Xavier University has played a large role in shaping Tom’s view of serving society by educating students to become rigorous and reflective; committed and compassionate individuals for and with others.

Rosemarie Molas — Vice President

Rose serves as the Vice President and is a member of the board of directors. In her VP role, she reports to the president and is responsible for the overall operational success of GHEO ensuring seamless team management and development, program delivery, and quality control and evaluation. She supports the president while ensuring alignment to GHEO’s mission and ensuring achievement of all major deliverables required to meet our goals.

Rose brings 25 years of experience as Information Technology Leader skilled in providing thought-leadership with a focus in executing global and speed-to-market strategies. She has a background serving the non-profit community in various positions. Education: Rose earned her B.S. in Computer Engineering, completed her M.S. in Information Technology, and is currently completing her MBA with a focus in Global Business from Lake Forest Graduate School of Management. She is also working on getting her Data Scientist Certification.

Amanda Ewertowski — Global Director Program Development

Amanda serves as our Corporate Development Director and is a member of the board of directors. She reports directly to our vice president and oversees all aspects of individual and corporate annual giving, planned gifts, and major gifts. She is an analytical thought leader with a customer-focused mindset who builds and sustains successful relationships with partners, vendors, and customers.

Amanda brings over 10 years of successful leadership experience in the Contract Management field. She is responsible for managing a $3.2 Billion dollar a year contract portfolio from end-to - end for Education, State & Local Government and Healthcare. Education: Amanda is a graduate of Texas A&M University, with a B.S. in Political Science. She also has her MBA from Lake Forest Graduate School of Management with a concentration in Organizational Behavior.

Craig Williamson — Global Director of Marketing & Communications

Craig serves as our Global Marketing and Communications Director and is a member of the board of directors. Reporting to the vice president, he will set and guide the strategy for all marketing and communications. He is responsible for delivering on GHEO’s organizational goals including brand development and the strategy and execution of all marketing campaigns and market presence.

Currently, Craig is a Program Manager at Walgreens Boots Alliance. He provides Program management support to the Integration Management Office within Walgreen Co. In this capacity, he works directly with program and project leads to ensure operational and capital resources are being allocated properly and all targets are being met for the executive operating committee.

Craig brings over 20 years of successful leadership experience in Operations, Portfolio Management, Supplier Negotiation, Leadership Development, Marketing and Communications.

Education: Craig received a B.S. from Northern Illinois University and his MBA from Lake Forest Graduate School of Management with a concentration in Global Marketing. He holds several other certifications in professional development including a PMP certificates in leadership and executive programs.

Craig is passionate about social responsibility and looks for opportunities to support national and global causes that improve lives, strengthen economies and grow strong community leaders.

Douglas B. Warren — Treasurer

Doug serves as our Treasurer and is a member of our Board of Directors. Reporting to the vice president, he will define the process and implement the infrastructure/systems needed to support substantial growth over the next five (5) to ten (10) years. Doug will continue to build and manage effective and streamlined administrative/financial systems, including financial, accounting, legal, information technology (IT), human resources (HR), and physical infrastructure. As a member of the senior management team, he will be involved in strategic planning, evaluation, and professional development initiatives, as well as planning for expansion globally.

Doug brings over 25 years of executive level experience in Commercial Banking and International Finance. His background also includes consulting and non-profit experience. He currently is a faculty member at Lake Forest Graduate School of Management, teaching Entrepreneurial Finance. Education: Douglas received a B.S. from the University of Chicago, his MBA in Finance and Economics from Kellogg School of Management and certification in non-profit management from the University of Illinois at Chicago.
